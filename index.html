How It Works

1

Create Your Identity

Generate an Ed25519 key pair. Your public key becomes your DID.

2

Start the Daemon

Run the local server. It stores your encrypted identity graph.

3

Connect AI Apps

Any AI app can read your context. You control what's shared.
